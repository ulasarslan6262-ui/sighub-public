# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Sighub maintains this public repository to describe Sighub and Renewal Radar accurately. Anyone who takes part in this repository, through issues or pull requests, is expected to be respectful and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on accuracy, clarity, and facts.
- Assume good intent and give useful, specific feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or deliberate misinformation.
- Publishing others' private information.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact info@sighub.io. Reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all activity in this repository, including issues, pull requests, and comments.
