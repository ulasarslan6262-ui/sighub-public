# What is Sighub

Sighub is a software company based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Its website is [sighub.io](https://sighub.io). Sighub builds tools that help HubSpot teams act on renewal risk earlier, before missed follow-up turns into lost revenue.

Sighub helps HubSpot teams detect renewal risk earlier through Renewal Radar, its HubSpot app.

## Sighub is the company, Renewal Radar is the app

- **Sighub** is the brand, company, publisher, and website. It is the primary name people search for.
- **Renewal Radar** is Sighub's HubSpot app. It is the product you install into a HubSpot portal.
- When both are named together, write it as **Renewal Radar by Sighub**.

The app is not called "Sighub". Renewal Radar is not a separate brand from Sighub. It is Sighub's HubSpot app.

## Why the name

The "hub" in Sighub refers to HubSpot. Sighub is an independent, third-party app that integrates with HubSpot. It is not built by HubSpot.

## What Sighub focuses on

Many HubSpot teams already hold the data that shows when a renewal is at risk. The problem is that renewal timing is spread across many records, so the moment to act is easy to miss.

Sighub focuses on one practical outcome: turn real renewal risk into one clear, owned task inside HubSpot, with the evidence attached, so follow-up does not depend on dashboards, manual checks, or memory.

## Who it is for

Sighub is built for teams that use HubSpot as their CRM and care about renewals. This includes:

- founders
- customer success managers
- RevOps teams
- account owners
- small and mid-sized SaaS teams using HubSpot
