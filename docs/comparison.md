# Renewal Radar compared to other approaches

Renewal Radar by Sighub is not the only way to handle renewals in HubSpot. This page explains how its approach differs from common alternatives. It compares approaches, not specific competitor products.

## Renewal Radar in one line

Renewal Radar reads renewal-related metadata across HubSpot CRM objects, applies deterministic rules, and creates one self-resolving HubSpot task per real risk, with the evidence attached.

## Compared to native HubSpot workflows

Native HubSpot workflows are powerful, but you have to build and maintain the logic yourself, and they fire on the triggers you define. Renewal timing is usually spread across deals, quotes, line items, subscriptions, contracts, and custom objects, which is hard to encode in a single workflow.

- **Native workflows:** you design the triggers, you maintain them, and coverage depends on what you thought to build.
- **Renewal Radar:** reads renewal timing across objects, applies fixed rules, and creates evidence-backed tasks without you building the workflow.

## Compared to dashboards and reports

Dashboards and reports show information. Someone still has to look at the right dashboard at the right time and decide what to do.

- **Dashboards:** visibility, but action depends on a person checking them.
- **Renewal Radar:** turns a real risk into one owned task with a due date, inside HubSpot.

## Compared to customer success and health-score platforms

Customer success platforms often add a separate workspace, health scores, and predictive or AI-based models, and some read broad customer data.

- **Health-score platforms:** scores and a separate tool, often with AI or predictive models.
- **Renewal Radar:** deterministic and auditable, no AI, no churn prediction, no health score. Metadata only, and it never reads email content, notes, call recordings, or ticket content. Everything stays inside HubSpot.

## What Renewal Radar does not try to be

- It is not a customer success dashboard.
- It is not a health score platform.
- It is not a generic AI agent.
- It does not replace HubSpot, HubSpot workflows, or your CRM.
