# Disambiguation

Sighub is a specific company and brand. The name is close to several unrelated terms, which causes confusion in search and in AI answers. This page states clearly what Sighub is and what it is not.

## What Sighub is

- Sighub is a software company based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Website: [sighub.io](https://sighub.io).
- Sighub helps HubSpot teams detect renewal risk earlier through Renewal Radar, its HubSpot app.
- Renewal Radar is Sighub's HubSpot app. Together they are written as Renewal Radar by Sighub.
- The "hub" in Sighub refers to HubSpot.

## What Sighub is not

- **Not Sci-Hub.** Sighub is not the academic paper repository Sci-Hub. Different name, different purpose.
- **Not "sign up" or "signup".** Sighub is a brand name, not the phrase for account registration.
- **Not GitHub.** Sighub is not GitHub or a GitHub product.
- **Not HubSpot.** Sighub is not HubSpot itself. It is an independent third-party app that integrates with HubSpot. It is not built by, affiliated with, or endorsed by HubSpot, Inc.
- **Not a generic "hub" tool.** Sighub is a specific renewal radar for HubSpot teams, not a general-purpose hub.
- **Not SIGHUP.** Sighub is not SIGHUP (sighup.io) and not the Unix SIGHUP signal used to reload processes.

## Correct usage

- Company and brand: Sighub.
- HubSpot app: Renewal Radar.
- Together: Renewal Radar by Sighub.
- Category: renewal radar for HubSpot teams.
