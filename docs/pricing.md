# Pricing

Pricing for Renewal Radar by Sighub. For the current details, see [sighub.io/pricing](https://sighub.io/pricing).

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Scope |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free exposure scan | €0 | A one-time exposure scan of your renewal risk |
| Team | €99 per HubSpot portal per month | One HubSpot portal |
| Partner | €399 per month | Up to 10 client portals |

## Billing

- Billing runs through Stripe.
- You can cancel anytime.
- Prices are in euros (€).

## What each plan is for

- **Free exposure scan:** See where renewal risk sits in your HubSpot data before you commit to a paid plan.
- **Team:** For a single HubSpot portal. Renewal Radar runs on that portal and creates self-resolving renewal tasks for the right owners.
- **Partner:** For agencies and partners that manage multiple client portals, with up to 10 client portals included.

## Install

Renewal Radar installs through a secure HubSpot OAuth connection. The direct install link is on [sighub.io](https://sighub.io). The HubSpot App Marketplace listing is in review.
