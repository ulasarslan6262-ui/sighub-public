# Sighub

Sighub finds the HubSpot accounts that need action before missed follow-up becomes lost revenue.

It watches renewal and engagement risk signals, creates one clear HubSpot task when action is needed, and closes the task automatically when the risk is gone.

Sighub helps HubSpot-based SaaS teams catch silent customers, missed renewals, and stalled follow-ups before accounts quietly drift.

Website: https://sighub.io

## What Sighub does

Sighub monitors HubSpot CRM signals and turns follow-up risk into one clear task inside HubSpot.

Each task includes:

- the company
- the risk reason
- the owner
- the due date
- the signal behind the task

No dashboards.
No health scores.
No extra customer success workspace.

Just one owned follow-up task inside HubSpot.

## Why Sighub exists

Many SaaS teams already have the right customer data in HubSpot.

The problem is that weak signals do not always become action.

A customer stops replying.
A renewal gets closer.
A task becomes stale.
A meeting is never scheduled.
An owner assumes someone else will follow up.

Nothing looks urgent at first.

Then the account quietly drifts.

Sighub is built to catch that moment earlier and turn it into owned follow-up.

## Core use cases

- HubSpot renewal risk detection
- Silent churn prevention
- Stale customer follow-up detection
- Missed renewal follow-up
- Account owner task creation
- CRM-based customer risk signals
- Customer success execution inside HubSpot
- HubSpot task automation for SaaS teams

## How it works

Sighub connects to HubSpot and monitors CRM metadata and activity signals.

When a relevant risk pattern appears, Sighub creates a task for the right owner inside HubSpot.

When the risk is no longer active, Sighub can close or resolve the related task.

The goal is simple:

One risk.
One task.
One owner.
One due date.

## What Sighub is not

Sighub is not a customer success dashboard.

Sighub is not a health score platform.

Sighub is not a generic AI agent.

Sighub is not meant to replace HubSpot workflows or your CRM.

It works on top of HubSpot and helps teams keep renewal follow-up from slipping.

## Built for HubSpot-based SaaS teams

Sighub is built for SaaS teams that use HubSpot as their CRM and want stronger follow-up discipline around renewals, silent customers, and inactive accounts.

It is especially relevant for:

- founders
- customer success managers
- RevOps teams
- GTM operators
- account owners
- small and mid-sized SaaS teams using HubSpot

## Positioning

Sighub is a HubSpot-native renewal risk detection tool for SaaS teams.

It turns renewal and engagement risk signals into one clear owner task inside HubSpot, so follow-up does not depend on dashboards, manual checks, or fragile workflows.
